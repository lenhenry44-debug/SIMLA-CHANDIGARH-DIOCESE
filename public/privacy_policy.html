Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Simla-Chandigarh Diocese" application ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information about you. This app is designed to provide spiritual resources, Mass readings, and hymns to our community.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal identifiable information (PII) from users. The application is designed to be used anonymously.

  • Voluntary Information: If you use the "Contact" feature to send us an email, we will receive your email address and any content you provide. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiries.

2. Third-Party Services

This application utilizes third-party services to generate and deliver content:

  • Google Gemini API: We use Google's generative AI to retrieve and format liturgical readings, saint biographies, and hymn lyrics. No personal user data is shared with Google for this purpose; only the request for specific religious content is sent.

3. Cookies and Tracking

This app does not use cookies for tracking purposes. It may use local storage on your device strictly to remember your preferred language settings (English, Hindi, or Punjabi) to enhance your user experience.

4. Children's Privacy

Our application is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us
